R.Chav Rebranding

Rebranded because I got tired of squinting at my own work and going,
“eh.”

Overview

Creative | Strategic | Versatile | Detail-Oriented | Storytelling

I am a designer who seamlessly blends traditional artistry with modern digital techniques. My portfolio showcases a diverse range of projects, from intricate hand-drawn illustrations to impactful rebranding initiatives. Each piece reflects a commitment to detail, creativity, and strategic thinking, aiming to craft visuals that resonate and tell compelling stories.

Brand Mission

To fuse artistic expression with strategic design, creating visuals that are both aesthetically pleasing and purpose-driven, ensuring each project not only captures attention but also communicates effectively.

I didn’t rebrand because I felt inspired. I rebranded because I looked at my old portfolio and genuinely couldn’t remember what I ever saw in it. It felt like a project I kept trying to make work out of loyalty, even after it gave me nothing back. At some point, I stood by it, probably even convinced myself it was fine. But eventually, I realized I’d outgrown it. So I scrapped it. This version still shifts. Still questions itself. But at least now, it sounds like me.

And if you’ve made it all the way down here, congrats on surviving my internal monologue dressed up as a design project.
